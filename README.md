# Island Survival Challenge
Frontend implementation based on the following API: https://island-survival-kit-builder.onrender.com/

You are stranded on a mysterious island with limited resources and no way off. Your mission is to survive by
gathering food and materials. You can craft tools to improve gathering and thereby increase your chances of
survival. You must manage your energy and what you gather efficiently. The ultimate goal is to gather enough
resources and craft a boat to escape the island.
